Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 November 2025

This privacy policy describes how the Story Stroll mobile application ("App") collects, uses and stores information.

1. Data we collect

  • The App does not require creating an account.
  • The App does not collect names, email addresses or other contact details.
  • The App does not access contacts, photos, microphone or location.

2. Analytics

We may collect anonymised technical information (such as device model, OS version, crash logs) to improve stability and performance of the App.

3. Third-party services

The App does not display third-party advertising and does not share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Children’s privacy

The App is not designed for children under 13 years old and does not knowingly collect their personal information.
